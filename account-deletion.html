How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Muse account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Muse app on your device
  2. Go to your profile by tapping your profile picture in the top right corner
  3. Tap "Settings" (gear icon)
  4. Scroll down and tap "Account"
  5. Select "Delete Account"
  6. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  7. Tap "Delete Account" to permanently remove your account
